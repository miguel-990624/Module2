My favourite Pokemons

Crobat

  • Weaknesses: Electric, Ice, Psychic, Rock
  • Ability (hidden): Infiltrator
  • Ability: Inner Focus
  • Category: Bat Pokémon
Crobat is so fast and silent that it often catches opponents off guard. Its four wings allow it to fly without making a sound, making it an expert at ambush attacks.

Scolepede

  • Weaknesses: Flying, Psychic, Fire, Rock
  • Ability (hidden): Speed Boost
  • Ability: Poison Point, Swarm
  • Category: Megapede Pokémon
Scolipede is an aggressive Bug/Poison Pokémon that charges at enemies with surprising speed. It uses the claws on its neck to grab prey and inject poison.

Gengar

  • Weaknesses: Ghost, Psychic, Dark
  • Ability (hidden): —
  • Ability: Cursed Body
  • Category: Shadow Pokémon
Gengar lurks in the shadows, stealing warmth and life force from nearby creatures. It's both mischievous and eerie, said to be a spirit that gained a body.

Metagross

  • Weaknesses: Fire, Ghost, Ground, Dark
  • Ability (hidden): Light Metal
  • Ability: Clear Body
  • Category: Iron Leg Pokémon
Metagross is a fusion of two Metang, giving it unmatched intelligence and computing power. It's a steel-armored beast that can crush foes with all four legs.

Krookodile

  • Weaknesses: Water, Ice, Grass, Fighting, Bug, Fairy
  • Ability (hidden): Anger Point
  • Ability: Intimidate, Moxie
  • Category: Intimidation Pokémon
Krookodile is a brutal desert predator with powerful jaws and excellent vision. It's known for using sneaky tactics in battle to ensure victory.

Luxray

  • Weaknesses: Ground
  • Ability (hidden): Guts
  • Ability: Rivalry, Intimidate
  • Category: Gleam Eyes Pokémon
Luxray has piercing eyesight that allows it to see through objects, making it excellent for locating hidden enemies. It's protective and brave, with a fierce electric charge.
