Kanto Starters, from pokemon gen 1

Charmander

  • Weaknesses: Water, Rock, Ground
  • Ability (hidden): Solar Power
  • Ability: Blaze
  • Category: Lizard Pokémon
Charmander has a flame on its tail that burns brighter when it's strong. It's a Fire-type Pokémon that's popular for its eventual evolution into Charizard.

Bulbasaur

  • Weaknesses: Fire, Ice, Flying, Psychic
  • Ability (hidden): Chlorophyll
  • Ability: Overgrow
  • Category: Seed Pokémon
Bulbasaur stores energy in the bulb on its back, which grows as it absorbs sunlight. It's both a Grass- and Poison-type, making it a balanced starter.

Squirtle

  • Weaknesses: Electric, Grass
  • Ability (hidden): Rain Dish
  • Ability: Torrent
  • Category: Tiny Turtle Pokémon
Squirtle withdraws into its shell to protect itself and sprays water with force. It's a solid Water-type choice with great defense.
